About HackKTP

Boston, MA - October 2023

Welcome to the first-ever HackKTP, the annual hackathon hosted by Kappa Theta Pi: Lambda. This event is dedicated to innovation, collaboration, and development within the tech industry.

Our fraternity understands the importance of utilizing the technology we have at our disposal to make a positive impact on the world. With that said, HackKTP is one of the many ways we give back to and empower members of our community.

As a hackathon, HackKTP is a space that draws multidisciplinary talent to solve complex problems and create powerful solutions. We believe the best ideas come from working together and learning from each other. Beyond collaboration and innovation, we encourage development. Development to us encompasses everything technical to everything professional, and we hope to foster a space perfect for networking as well as adequately provide the technical resources so our participants can succeed.

HackKTP will take place in the middle of the fall semester, and we welcome all Boston University students to pre-register for the hackathon using the form below. Whether you’re an experienced coder or a newcomer to the tech industry, HackKTP is the perfect opportunity to collaborate with like-minded individuals and work on innovative projects.

We also would like to extend an invitation to organizations interested in sponsoring our event to reach out to us via the button below. As a sponsor, you will have the opportunity to connect with a talented pool of students and support this student-led event.

Thank you for considering HackKTP and we look forward to seeing you at our next event!

Pillars

Innovation

Hackathons across the globe harness and incubate talent by bringing in motivated young minds to a space that's perfectly tuned for technological creativity. We hope Hackappathon will be no different, and challenge you, push you, and excite you more than anything to innovate.

Collaboration

It's no doubt that events like these bring in a talented, multidisciplined, and diverse set of minds to challenge the toughest problems. Doing so though requires collaboration and teamwork. We at Kappa Theta Pi are dedicated to creating a collaborative space that's tailored to helping you succeed.

Development

Often too much focus is put on the technical aspects of these kinds of events, but here it's of the utmost importance to promote networking, teamwork, and presentation skills. We do this though, not by ignoring the importance of workshops and technical support. We focus on personal development in general.

FAQ

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is an innovation marathon. Students come together to build innovative software and hardware hacks over the course of 24-48 hours. The event's like these are designed to be beginner friendly and participation is open to all majors.

How much does it cost to attend?

Totally free, but pre-registration or applications are necessary to attend.

Do I need to be a brother of Kappa Theta Pi to attend?

As of right now to participate in HackKTP you need to be a student of Boston University, but you do not need to be a brother!

Is there a code of conduct?

Yes, it can be found here.

Who is Kappa Theta Pi?

Kappa Theta Pi is a professional co-ed technology fraternity. We were founded in 2011 at the University of Michigan. If you want to learn more about this particular chapter, please go over to our website ktpbostonu.com.

Is Hackappathon looking for sponsors for this event?

Yes, Hackappathon is currently accepting sponsors for the event. If you are interested in becoming a sponsor, please contact us at ktpbostonu@gmail.com. We would be happy to provide you with more details about sponsorship opportunities and benefits.

What kind of sponsorship participation is Hackappathon looking for?

At Hackappathon we welcome all types of sponsors who are interested in supporting our event and helping to create a valuable experience for our participants. If you would like to discuss sponsorship packages please contact us at ktpbostonu@gmail.com.
