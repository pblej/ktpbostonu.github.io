Rush




Spring Rush 2023 Timeline


Open House #1 and #2

Rush will begin with 2 open houses. At the open house, we will give you an overview of the fraternity and introduce you to our E-Board. You will be able to introduce yourself to the brothers as well as the other attendees. For rush, it is only required for you to attend one open house.

Design Jam

During rush, we will host a design jam, attendees will be split up into small groups and work on a marketable solution to a given problem. Then at the end of the night teams will present what they came up with to the judges and their peers. This event is meant to develop problem-solving and professional presentation skills.

Meet and Greet

Small groups of prospective candidates will rotate through conversations with each active brother. The brothers will go into depth about who they are and why they are a part of KTP. As well as ask questions and engage in conversation with the prospective candidates.

Coffee Chats

Prospective candidates will have to schedule 1 coffee chat with a brother, these chats will be about 30 minutes of getting to know each other and general bonding. Coffee chats are a crucial part of the rush process and a time in which a prospective candidate can really get to know an active.

Community Service

Having a positive impact in your community is something that Kappa Theta Pi is excited to honor. Being a part of such a strong community as Boston we recognize that giving back is important and we expect our prospective candidates to understand this and join us for some community service.

Interviews

We want to know who you are! Are you a good fit for Kappa Theta Pi? Do you value innovation technology? Are you excited about Data? Do you value our pillars? We want to know the answers to these questions, and we are excited to hear from you and hope you are excited about this opportunity!



Frequently Asked Questions




In order to qualify for rush, you must adhere to the following guidelines: (1) You must have at least three semesters remaining on campus. (2) You must not be apart of any other proessional fraternities. (3) And you must do your best to attend KTP events! Rush is supposed to be fun and engaging! We accept all majors and anyone interested at Boston University.



At KTP we try to make pledging a fun and beneficial experience. We believe that our pledging process is not intense and can accommodate most schedules. What you put into it is what you get out of it!



As of right now we believe rush will start the week of Janurary 24th, keep up-to-date about KTP events on the KTP instagram!
